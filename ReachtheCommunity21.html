About

IEEE Reach the Community 2021

The main theme of the event is to build future leaders from the younger generation and to improve the memberships, So it is specially focused on helping students to utilise IEEE platform to exhibit their expertise by participating in various competitions and contests in both technical and non-technical fields. IEEE Reach the Community 2021 is an opportunity to get the better. It is a platform with various opportunities where the legendries of IEEE R10,IEEE Section Executive committee members, 16 Different chapter chairs, and many senior member of IEEE Hyderabad Section and professors are invited to get connected virtually and aware you on different concepts which helps you for future competitions conducted by Organized under IEEE R10 Membership Development and Leadership Training Initiative 2021 by IEEE Vardhaman College of Engineering SB, IEEE SVCE SB, IEEE SVEC SB, IEEE Narayana Engineering College Nellore SB Sponsored by IEEE R10 and IEEE R10 SAC
