Message from IEEE ES FACULTY ADVISOR


IEEE is the largest professional organization in the world and helps its members access to network with the best brains in cutting edge areas. I joined IEEE a decade back since then I have gained a lot through IEEE memberships and its daughter societies such as Computer society, Education Society Membership, Electron Devices Society Membership, Big Data Community, Block chain Community, Cloud Computing Community, Cyber security Community, Data Engineering, IEEE Computer Society Technical Committee on and etc.

My volunteering activities started as the student branch counselor in 2007 and organized various activities and got applauds from IEEE .That prompted me to do more and I became the secretary of IEEE CS chapter Hyderabad section. Sincerely then I have participated in several activities and conferences including general chair, Reviewer for IEEE Access Journal and Session chair for several IEEE Conferences. I also became the Vice chair, IEEE CS Chapter Hyderabad section. This shows that if we are committed to IEEE, we can grow very quickly. Moreover these roles have helped me interact with several top researchers and experts in our filed on regular basis.

With this motivation, I encouraged many colleges and started IEEE student branches and societies . Under IEEE I have organized various career guidance programs for Scholl children also.

Overall my career has been a steep growth towards IEEE and its societies. I am sure that without my IEEE membership and Volunteering, I would not have been able to achieve that I have done in such a short time.

- Dr. M. A. Jabbar,
   IEEE ES, Student Chapter Advisor, Vardhaman College of Engineering.
