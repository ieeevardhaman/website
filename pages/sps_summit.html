About the event

The Biomedical Signals and Deep Learning has numerous applications in healthcare—from remote monitoring and assistive technologies to smart sensors in wearable’s and medical device integration. It has the potential to keep patients safe and healthy and also to improve the capability of physicians as well and also creates new possibilities for healthcare delivery. In this Regional Meeting, the instructors will cover the topics of Biomedical Signal Processing and Healthcare Deep Learning applications, presenting the most recent advances of research in these fields and providing the attendees with the necessary skills to address the future challenges related to biomedical engineering and healthcare.

IEEE Signal Processing Regional Meeting on Deep Learning and Biomedical Signals 2021, is the First edition of regional meeting organized by IEEE Signal Processing Society Student Chapter under IEEE Vardhaman Student Branch in association with IEEE Vardhaman SB WIE, Computer Society and Education Society Chapter on 14th – 16th June 2021, it is the Student Professional Awareness Conference (SPAC) 2021, which is Being funded by IEEE Signal Processing Society and IEEE Students, Technical consortium that encourages women and men in engineering disciplines to share their ideas and build up a professional network to enhance their skills.

IEEE Vardhaman Student Branch under Signal Processing Society has the ability to bring formidable IEEE and IEEE SPS Members together from across the globe and the potential to make a shift change in engineering cultures worldwide. The Theme of the Event " Deep Learning and Biomedical Signals "demonstrates the strength arising from working together as a community which surely can mark a significant change within us before this year comes to an end.

We the members enthusiastically and passionately coming forward to work with you all and to serve you in coming months. Regional SPS Summit will make the students aware about multitude of skill sets, powerful mind development, conductive learning environment, and networking opportunities and provides a chance to grow globally.

    Through this event,

  • Members can connect with people worldwide, share and exchange knowledge, work as a team, strengthening networks.
  • The Track which focuses on women empowerment in domains of Biomedical Signals and Deep Learning in Healthcare and help women advance in their careers and encourage IEEE Members in their development goals.
  • This summit will focus more than ever on impact and deliverables, have become passionate and enthusiastic about the progress and visibility of IEEE SPS in IEEE.

We would love to meet every member of IEEE SPS this year, and We would like to invite you to join us to contribute and make a change, boost their self-confidence and prove that women should be independent and reach their goals even in the hardest fields of Biomedical Signals and Deep Learning.
The programme is supported by IEEE Signal processing society. This regional meeting is mainly focusing towards creating interest and motivating the participants in the area Biomedical and Deep Learning domains in health care applications.
