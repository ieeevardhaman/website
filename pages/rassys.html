About

RAS Symposium

IEEE VARDHAMAN COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING Robotics and Automation Society Symposium 2021 is a global platform to discuss and learn about Artificial Intelligence, robotics, Big Data, Artificial Intelligence, Automation and many more trends in the field of Robotics and Automation. The RAS Symposium 2021 program includes the below: B2B Meetings, Panel Discussions, Industry expert interactions, Round table meetings, Best Poster awards, and Best Oral presentation awards, Young Researcher Forums, e-Poster presentations, Video presentations by the experts from both Industry & Academic.

Why

RAS Symposium

IEEE VARDHAMAN COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING Robotics and Automation Society Symposium 2021 brings together experts, leading researchers, scholars, scientists, professors from fields of Physics, and other related sectors to interact and interchange ideas about the situation of the art technologies related to Artificial intelligence, Robotics and Internet-related things. This conference will also provide an insightful understanding to the problems arising out of the Artificial Intelligence and Robotics. This Symposium provides an Opportunity to communicate with prominent Scientists, researchers, Business Leaders, experts from all over the world. The little effort put by the Robotics summit 2021 will help us in taking a big step in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, mechatronics and Internet of Things.
